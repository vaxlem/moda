1. BIOgraphy

Biography of the late Cinderella

The late Mrs Cinderella Mawunegbloe Sosoo affectionately called SUKUTOR by her peers was born in the year 1958 to her late parents, Dumega David Yao Sedzoame Sosu and Madam AKuyovi Nukunu at Anloga - Agorve. Mawunegbloe Cinde was the 8th child of her parents. Her mother who was a disciplinarian trained her in all kinds of business including home chores. These trainings made her a versatile woman from the family business of fish dressing, marketing, farming and trading in abolo, yakayake and fish among others.

After a few years she became independent soul career taking care of her immediate family especially children of her older siblings.

She traded at markets such as Akuse, Ada and Anloga markets. She worked hard in life to sustain her family. Mawunegbloe Cinderella, loves school and her determination to get to the top earned her the nickname SUKUTOR.

Growing into a very respectful lady, she started her basic education from class one at Agorve L/A and was always among the 1st top 10 scorers. During her school days she was slim and easy going that her mates called her "Kaketor /Agbahodzoe." Help was needed so she suspended her schooling to take care of her sibling's children. After a while she returned to school to complete form 4 at the Dornorgbor L/A school. From there, she continued to Anseco where she studied arts. She loved to sing and took part in Ep church choir and school drama groups. She has a dream of becoming a nurse, so, in 1984 and 1985, She wrote these 2 entrance exams and passed but she couldn’t go to the training.

She got admissions into the nursing college twice at the time, but unfortunately, her dream was truncated to caretaking job of her niece nephews and cousins. Thus, sadly her dream of becoming a registered nurse was shuttered in the 1984 and 85. Hard-working as she was, she moved on to engaged in abolo business alongside petty trading and finally settled for corn business.

She was skillful and lent sewing as a vocation which she did till her final days on earth. She loves gardening, singing, cooking, wearing smiles, and above all caring for all manner of persons. She is loved by all especially her in-laws. Even those who never met her in person has admirations for what ideal woman she had been. At a point in time, she moved to Accra Mamobi to engage in abolo business where she met her husband whom she later married to and gave birth to her beautiful children. Mrs. CINDERELLA MAWUNEGBLOE SOSOO took ill for so many years and hoping to recover but unfortunately loss it all. She gave up her last breath to rest after a prolonged battle for survival, on the faithful day of Saturday December 21st 2024. She was 66 when leaving behind her loved ones. Cinderella Mawunegbloe keep resting till we meet again. God loves you
