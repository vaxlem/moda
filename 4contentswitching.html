Biography of the late Cinderella

"

CINDERELLA SI MEGALI O ƑE AGBEMEŊUTINYA.

"+

Wodzi Aƒenɔ Cinderella Mawunegbloe Sosoo si megali o, si ehatiwo yɔna lɔlɔ̃tɔe be SUKUTOR, le ƒe 1958 me, na edzilawo siwo megali o, Dumega David Yao Sedzoame Sosu kple Madam AKuyovi Nukunu. Wodzii le Anloga - Agorve. Mawunegbloe Cinde nye edzilawo ƒe vi 8 lia. Dadaa si nye amehehela la na hehee le asitsatsa ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe me, eye aƒemedɔwo hã le eme. Hehe siawo na wòzu nyɔnu si te ŋu wɔa nu vovovowo tso ƒomea ƒe asitsatsa si nye tɔmelãwo ƒoƒo, asitsatsa, agbledede kple asitsatsa le abolo, yakayake, kalami kple bubuawo me. Le ƒe ʋee aɖewo megbe la, eva zu ame ɖeka kolia si le eɖokui si, eye wòkpɔa eƒe ƒometɔ kplikplikpliwo dzi vevietɔ nɔvia tsitsiwo ƒe viwo. Etsaa asi le asiwo abe Akuse, Ada, kple Anloga asiwo ene me. Eku kutri vevie le agbe me be yealé yeƒe ƒomea ɖe te. Mawunegbloe Cinderella lɔ̃a sukudede, eye eƒe tameɖoɖo kplikpaa be yeaɖo ta la na wotsɔ ŋkɔ nɛ be SUKUTOR.



Esi wòtsi va zu nyɔnu si dea bubu ame ŋu ŋutɔ la, edze eƒe gɔmedzesukudede gɔme tso klass gbãtɔ me le Agorve L/A eye wònɔa 1st top 10 scorers dome ɣesiaɣi. Le eƒe sukudeɣiwo la, enye ame wusewuse si ʋaa na kabakaba ale gbegbe be eƒe zɔhɛawo yɔe be 'Kaketor /Agbahodzoe'. Kpekpeɖeŋu va hiã, eyata edzudzɔ eƒe sukudede be yeakpɔ nɔvia ƒe viwo dzi. Le ɣeyiɣi aɖe megbe la, etrɔ yi suku be yeakpe form 4 lia ɖo le Dornorgbor L/A sukume. Tso afima la, eyi Anseco afisi wòsrɔ̃ aɖaŋudɔwo le. Elɔ̃a hadzidzi eye wòkpɔa gome le EP sɔlemeha ƒe hadziha kple suku ƒe drama ƒuƒoƒowo me. Eƒe drɔ̃e kukue nye be yeazu dɔnɔdzikpɔla, eyata, le ƒe 1984 kple 1985 me la, ewɔ ƒe 2 siawo ƒe dɔnɔdzikpɔkpɔ ƒe kɔledzi dodokpɔwo le ɣeyiɣi ɖeka me eye wòto eme nyuie.

Ekpɔ mɔ hena gege ɖe dɔnɔdzikpɔlawo ƒe kɔledzia zi eve ɣemaɣi wo, gake nublanuitɔe la, woɖe eƒe drɔ̃ea dzi kpɔtɔ he va zu eƒe tɛyɔviwo, kple nɔviyɔviwo ƒe belélenamedɔ wɔ la. Eyata nublanuitɔe la, wotu eƒe drɔ̃e be yeazu dɔnɔdzikpɔla si woŋlɔ ŋkɔ na le ƒe 1984 kple 85 me. Togbɔ be enye dɔsesẽwɔla hã la, eyi ŋgɔ va ƒo eɖokui ɖe abolo ƒe asitsatsa me kpe ɖe asitsatsa suesuesuewo ŋu eye mlɔeba wòva nɔ bli ƒe asitsatsa me. "

Ebi ɖe edzi eye wòsrɔ Tela abe yɔyɔ ene si wòwɔ vaseɖe eƒe ŋkeke mamlɛawo dzi le anyigba dzi. Elɔ̃a agbledzikpɔkpɔ, hadzidzi, nuɖaɖa, alɔgbɔnukoko dodo, eye ƒo wo katã ta la, beléle na ame ƒomevi ɖesiaɖe. Amewo katã lɔ̃nɛ vevietɔ eƒe lɔxoyɔviwo kple nyoyɔviwo. Amesiwo medo goe ŋutɔŋutɔ kpɔ o gɔ̃ hã kpɔ dzidzɔ ɖe nyɔnu nyui si wònye tsã ŋu. Le ɣeyiɣi aɖe me la, eʋu yi Juapon eye emegbe wòʋu yi Accra - Mamobi be yeaƒo ye ɖokui ɖe Abolo ƒe asitsatsa me, afimae wòdo go srɔ̃a le, amesi wòɖe emegbe eye wòdzi vi nyakpɔwo. Aƒenɔ CINDERELLA MAWUNEGBLOE SOSOO - TETTEY, dze dɔ ƒe geɖe eye wònɔ mɔ kpɔm be yehaya gake nublanuitɔe la, ebu wo katã. Eɖe asi le eƒe gbɔgbɔ mamlɛtɔ ŋu be yeayi aɖaɖi ɖe eme le agbetsitsi ƒe aʋa didi aɖe megbe le nuteƒewɔwɔŋkeke si nye Memleɖa, December 21st, 2024 dzi, le esime wòxɔ ƒe 66. Cinderella Mawunegbloe, Mawu lɔ̃ wò. Yi edzi nànɔ ɖiɖim ɖe eme vaseɖe esime míagado go ake.
